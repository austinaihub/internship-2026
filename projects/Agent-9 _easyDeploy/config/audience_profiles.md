# Audience Profiles — Human Trafficking Awareness Campaign

Use this reference to match news content to the most relevant target audience.
Select the audience whose **Trigger Keywords** best align with the story.
Use **Anti-keywords** to rule out near-miss audiences when topics overlap.

---

## college_students

- **Trigger Keywords:** campus, university, college, student, dorm, fraternity, sorority, dating app, sextortion, online grooming, peer recruitment, scholarship fraud, foreign student labor, spring break
- **Anti-keywords:** *(Do NOT select this audience if the story centers on supply chain compliance, legislative policy, or parental monitoring tools)*
- **Tone:** Direct, punchy, social-media-native. Short sentences, rhetorical questions. Speak as a peer.
- **Focus:** Recognizing warning signs in peers, online exploitation, sextortion, coercive relationships disguised as romance, labor exploitation of international students.
- **CTA:** "Share this post" / "Tag someone who needs to see this" / "Save this for later"
- **Preferred Style Preset:** `rembrandt` or `cinematic_depth`
- **Visual Style:** Dark background with a single neon accent color. High contrast, shallow depth of field. Mood: urgent, intimate. Minimal elements — one subject, lots of negative space.

---

## educators

- **Trigger Keywords:** school, teacher, classroom, curriculum, safeguarding, training program, mandatory reporting, youth program, school counselor, prevention education, campus policy
- **Anti-keywords:** *(Do NOT select if the story is about parental tools/family dialogue or about university-age students — use `parents` or `college_students` instead)*
- **Tone:** Professional, informative, actionable. Assume the reader can implement change in their institution.
- **Focus:** Teaching awareness in K-12 settings, recognizing behavioral signs in students, mandatory reporting, building safe school environments, educator training.
- **CTA:** "Download the resource guide" / "Bring this to your next staff meeting" / "Train your team"
- **Preferred Style Preset:** `editorial_flat` or `fog_silence`
- **Visual Style:** Warm natural tones (cream, soft amber). Soft diffused light. Mood: quiet, contemplative. Clean editorial feel — one focal point with generous negative space.

---

## business_owners

- **Trigger Keywords:** supply chain, forced labor, factory, manufacturing, ESG, compliance, audit, sourcing, Modern Slavery Act, UFLPA, agriculture, construction, hospitality, fishing, domestic work, garment, warehouse, contractor, visa sponsorship
- **Anti-keywords:** *(Do NOT select if the story is about online/sexual exploitation or school-based prevention — use a different audience)*
- **Tone:** Business-oriented, compliance-focused, data-driven. Frame trafficking as operational risk and legal liability.
- **Focus:** Supply chain due diligence, forced labor indicators across industries (garment, agriculture, construction, hospitality, fishing), legal liability, ethical sourcing, worker recruitment practices.
- **CTA:** "Audit your suppliers" / "Review your compliance framework" / "Request a supply chain assessment"
- **Preferred Style Preset:** `editorial_flat` or `rembrandt`
- **Visual Style:** Cool blue-steel tones. Sharp geometric lines, tungsten lighting. Mood: authoritative, restrained. Minimal composition — single subject against clean industrial or architectural background.

---

## parents

- **Trigger Keywords:** family, parental control, grooming, predator, teen, child online safety, app monitoring, social media risk, recruitment of minors, runaway, missing youth
- **Anti-keywords:** *(Do NOT select if the story is about school policy/educator training — use `educators`. Do NOT select if it centers on college-age adults — use `college_students`)*
- **Tone:** Emotionally resonant, protective, conversational. Parent-to-parent. Empower with actionable steps, avoid fear-mongering.
- **Focus:** Online safety at home, recognizing grooming patterns, open family dialogue, age-appropriate conversations, knowing what apps children use.
- **CTA:** "Talk to your child tonight" / "Check their privacy settings" / "Know the apps they use"
- **Preferred Style Preset:** `cinematic_depth` or `fog_silence`
- **Visual Style:** Golden hour warmth, soft bokeh. Palette: amber and muted cream. Mood: protective, tender. One intimate detail or silhouette with expansive soft background.

---

## lawmakers

- **Trigger Keywords:** legislation, bill, policy, senate, congress, regulation, task force, federal, treaty, prosecution reform, sentencing, funding, interagency, international cooperation, UN protocol, government report
- **Anti-keywords:** *(Do NOT select if the story is a single arrest/conviction with no policy implication — use `general_public`. Select this ONLY when the story has a clear legislative, regulatory, or systemic policy dimension)*
- **Tone:** Formal, data-heavy, policy-oriented. Cite specific legal gaps and international comparisons. Assume the reader influences policy.
- **Focus:** Legislative gaps, sentencing reform, funding for survivor services, cross-border cooperation frameworks, gaps between state and federal law.
- **CTA:** "Contact your representative" / "Support this bill" / "Demand stronger enforcement"
- **Preferred Style Preset:** `rembrandt` or `fog_silence`
- **Visual Style:** Desaturated, near-monochrome documentary grade. Harsh directional light. Mood: grave, formal. Stark composition — one subject isolated in frame with deep shadow.

---

## general_public

- **Trigger Keywords:** *(default fallback — use when no other audience is a strong match, OR when the story has broad human-interest appeal: a single arrest/conviction, a rescue operation, a survivor story, community awareness)*
- **Anti-keywords:** *(none — this is the catch-all)*
- **Tone:** Accessible, thought-provoking, human-centered. Assume no prior knowledge. Lead with a surprising fact.
- **Focus:** Basic awareness, key statistics, recognizing signs in everyday life, hotline information, how trafficking happens in ordinary places.
- **CTA:** "Call the National Hotline: 1-888-373-7888" / "Learn the signs" / "Report suspicious activity"
- **Preferred Style Preset:** `cinematic_depth` (default)
- **Visual Style:** Cinematic teal-and-orange grade, dramatic chiaroscuro. Mood: raw, contemplative. One powerful subject with expansive negative space — photojournalism simplicity.
