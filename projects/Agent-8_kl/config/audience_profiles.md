# Audience Profiles — Human Trafficking Awareness Campaign

Use this reference to match news content to the most relevant target audience.
Select the audience whose **Trigger Keywords** and **Focus** best align with the story.

---

## 🎓 college_students

- **Trigger Keywords:** campus, university, young, online, social media, dorm, student, fraternity, sorority, dating app, sextortion
- **Tone:** Direct, punchy, social-media-native. Use rhetorical questions and short sentences. Speak as a peer, not an authority.
- **Focus:** Recognizing warning signs in peers, online exploitation risks, sextortion, coercive relationships disguised as romance.
- **CTA:** "Share this post" / "Tag someone who needs to see this" / "Save this for later"
- **Visual Style:** Vibrant neon accents on dark background, bold chunky sans-serif typography, street-art / graffiti texture, high saturation highlight colors, Instagram-native 4:5 aspect ratio, phone-screen-native framing.

---

## 👩‍🏫 educators

- **Trigger Keywords:** school, children, training, prevention, classroom, teacher, curriculum, safeguarding, minor, youth program
- **Tone:** Professional, informative, actionable. Assume the reader has authority to implement change in their institution.
- **Focus:** Teaching awareness in classrooms, recognizing behavioral signs in students, mandatory reporting obligations, building safe environments.
- **CTA:** "Download the resource guide" / "Bring this to your next staff meeting" / "Train your team"
- **Visual Style:** Clean editorial layout, warm natural tones (beige, cream, soft green), soft window light, classroom/library/school hallway setting, Kodak Portra warmth, serif or clean sans-serif typography.

---

## 🏢 business_owners

- **Trigger Keywords:** labor, supply chain, industry, workforce, factory, manufacturing, corporate, compliance, ESG, forced labor, exploitation
- **Tone:** Business-oriented, compliance-focused, data-driven. Frame trafficking as a business risk and legal liability.
- **Focus:** Supply chain due diligence, forced labor indicators, legal liability (UFLPA, Modern Slavery Act), ethical sourcing, industry-specific red flags.
- **CTA:** "Audit your suppliers" / "Review your compliance framework" / "Request a supply chain assessment"
- **Visual Style:** Corporate blue-steel palette, sharp geometric composition, boardroom/factory/warehouse setting, cool tungsten lighting, clean Helvetica typography, minimal and authoritative.

---

## 👨‍👩‍👧 parents

- **Trigger Keywords:** family, children, online safety, predator, grooming, teen, parental control, app, social media risks, recruitment
- **Tone:** Emotionally resonant, protective, conversational. Speak parent-to-parent. Avoid fear-mongering; empower with actionable steps.
- **Focus:** Online safety, recognizing grooming patterns, open family dialogue, age-appropriate conversations, monitoring without surveillance.
- **CTA:** "Talk to your child tonight" / "Check their privacy settings" / "Know the apps they use"
- **Visual Style:** Warm domestic lighting, soft bokeh, home environment objects (phone on a kitchen table, empty chair at a desk, backpack by a door), golden hour warmth, gentle color palette (amber, cream, muted rose).

---

## 🏛️ lawmakers

- **Trigger Keywords:** legislation, bill, policy, government, law, senate, congress, regulation, task force, federal, state-level, treaty, prosecution
- **Tone:** Formal, data-heavy, policy-oriented. Cite specific legal gaps and international comparisons. Assume the reader influences policy.
- **Focus:** Legislative gaps, sentencing reform, funding for survivor services, cross-border cooperation, gaps between state and federal law.
- **CTA:** "Contact your representative" / "Support this bill" / "Demand stronger enforcement"
- **Visual Style:** Desaturated documentary color grade, government architecture (marble columns, wood-paneled courtrooms), official seals and documents, harsh fluorescent overhead or dramatic spotlight, stencil or formal serif typography.

---

## 🌍 general_public

- **Trigger Keywords:** *(default fallback — use when no other audience is a strong match)*
- **Tone:** Accessible, thought-provoking, human-centered. Assume no prior knowledge. Lead with a surprising fact.
- **Focus:** Basic awareness, key global statistics, recognizing signs in everyday life, hotline information, how trafficking happens in ordinary places.
- **CTA:** "Call the National Hotline: 1-888-373-7888" / "Learn the signs" / "Report suspicious activity"
- **Visual Style:** Cinematic teal-and-orange color grade, dramatic chiaroscuro lighting, high-contrast bold sans-serif typography, gritty urban texture (concrete, weathered walls), photojournalism-meets-poster aesthetic.
